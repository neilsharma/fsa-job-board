## About This Position

Join our DevOps team to help build and maintain the infrastructure that powers critical education systems serving millions of users. You'll work on cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and security compliance in a mission-driven environment.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and maintain cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, or cloud.gov)
- Build and improve CI/CD pipelines for automated deployments
- Implement infrastructure as code using tools like Terraform or CloudFormation
- Monitor system performance and ensure high availability
- Implement security controls and ensure compliance with federal standards
- Support development teams with platform and tooling improvements
- Participate in incident response and root cause analysis

## Qualifications

### Required
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or equivalent experience
- 4+ years of experience in DevOps, SRE, or systems engineering
- Strong experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud)
- Proficiency with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Experience with infrastructure as code (Terraform, Ansible, or similar)
- Knowledge of CI/CD tools (GitHub Actions, Jenkins, GitLab CI, or similar)
- Understanding of networking, security, and system architecture

### Preferred
- Experience with federal cloud environments (cloud.gov, AWS GovCloud)
- Knowledge of federal security compliance (FedRAMP, FISMA, NIST 800-53)
- Experience with monitoring and observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK)
- Scripting proficiency (Python, Bash, or similar)
- Experience with database administration and performance tuning
- Security certifications (Security+, CISSP, or similar)

## Work Environment

This position offers flexibility to work remotely or hybrid from our Washington, DC office. Some positions may require the ability to obtain security clearance.
